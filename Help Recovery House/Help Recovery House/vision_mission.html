About Us

  • Vision

    To be one of the Middle East leaders in Providing effective and affordable addiction recovery treatment, Teaching relevant life and job skills, Ensuring the best quality of life for our partners

  • Mission

    To give those who are committed to change their lives the opportunity for lifelong balance and a happy successful life & be beside them whenever needed & help them to be eligible, active & effective citizens ( in their country)

  • Our Values

    We believe in God, we believe in ourselves. We uphold the dignity of each individual. We believe in humility, forgiveness, humanity, learning, responsibility, service, and to be self-sufficient
